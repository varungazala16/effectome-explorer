What is induced proximity?

Small bifunctional molecules bring two proteins into close proximity, enabling one to modify the other — through degradation, activation, or re-localization.

🔗

What is the effectome?

The full set of effector proteins capable of modulating a disease target via proximity events. Mapping it is the first step in proximity drug discovery.

🎯

Why undruggable targets?

~85% of the proteome lacks a binding pocket for conventional drugs. Proximity-based approaches bypass this by co-opting the cell's own machinery.
